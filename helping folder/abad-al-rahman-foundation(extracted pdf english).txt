"Together We Create Impact"

Who are we and what do we want?
A non-profit charitable foundation working since 2007 to support the poor and needy and develop rural communities through sustainable food, medical, and development programs.

Our goals in brief:
* Medical Care: Providing comprehensive and free medical services through the Medical Complex for every needy person.
* Food Security: Sustaining feeding and developing the "Al-Namrout Kitchen" to reach thousands of families.
* Orphan Sponsorship: Caring for modest families and orphans financially and morally to ensure a decent life.
* Building Impact: Establishing the new Medical Complex (6 floors) as a sustainable ongoing charity.
* Village Development: Advancing public services in Al-Namrout and supporting the marriage of those unable.

Our Vision:
* Leadership: To be the first model in providing free medical care with global quality.
* Inclusivity: A society recovered in health and self-sufficient in food.
* Sustainability: Transforming donations into permanent charitable assets.
* Dignity: Providing aid to every needy person with the highest standards of modesty and appreciation.

Our Activities and Fields of Work:

First: Al-Namrout Charity Kitchen (Ramadan)
* Meal cost: 65 EGP.
* Cooking 25,000 meals throughout the month of Ramadan.
* Daily distribution right to the doors of the deserving.
* Distribution covers more than 10 villages and hamlets.
* We deliver goodness with dignity... to where the need resides.
* 8 years.. and the goodness is still flowing.

Secondly: Al-Namrout Charity Pharmacy
* Dispensing free medication for the poor and needy all year round.
* Average monthly cost: 50,000 EGP.
* Medicine is a right for every needy person.
* Ibad Al-Rahman Pharmacy: 8 years of providing safe and free medicine.

Thirdly: The Charity Medical Complex in Al-Namrout Village
It provides all its services for free to those unable and includes:
* Incubators.
* General surgery center.
* Medical laboratory.
* More than 13 specialized clinics.
* Ongoing charity serving generations.
* Comprehensive medical care with no charge.
* The Medical Complex: 10 years of excellence in free healthcare...

Fourthly: Fixed monthly aid
* Supporting more than 25 families.
* With a monthly cost of: 20,000 EGP.

Fifthly: General assistance
Providing urgent aid according to the needs of the cases:
* Treatment.
* Medical devices.
* Living support.
* Emergency needs.
