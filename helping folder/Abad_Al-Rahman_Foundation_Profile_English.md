# Abad Al-Rahman Charity Foundation for Community Development
*Registered under No. 1784 for the year 2007*

> **"Together We Make An Impact"**

---

## 1. Who Are We and What Do We Want?
A non-profit charity foundation working since 2007 to support the poor and needy, and to develop rural communities through sustainable food, medical, and development programs.

## 2. Our Vision
- **Leadership:** To be the premier model in providing free medical services with global quality.
- **Comprehensiveness:** To build a community that is healthy and self-sufficient in food.
- **Sustainability:** To transform donations into permanent charitable assets (Sadaqah Jariyah) serving future generations.
- **Dignity:** To provide aid to every needy person with the highest standards of modesty and appreciation.

## 3. Our Goals in Lines
- **Medical Care:** Providing free and comprehensive medical services (via the medical complex) to everyone in need.
- **Food Security:** Sustaining food distribution and developing the "Al-Namrouth Kitchen" to reach thousands of families.
- **Orphan Sponsorship:** Caring for modest families and orphans financially and morally to ensure a decent life.
- **Building Impact:** Establishing the new medical complex (6 floors) as a continuous charity (Sadaqah Jariyah).
- **Village Development:** Advancing public services in Al-Namrouth and supporting the marriage of those unable to afford it.

---

## 4. Our Activities and Fields of Work

### First: Al-Namrouth Charity Kitchen (Ramadan)
*We deliver goodness with dignity... to where the need resides.*
* *"8 years... and the goodness is still flowing"*
- **Meal Cost:** 65 EGP per meal.
- **Volume:** 25,000 meals are cooked throughout the month of Ramadan.
- **Distribution:** Daily distribution delivered directly to the beneficiaries' doors, covering more than 10 villages and hamlets.

### Second: Al-Namrouth Charity Pharmacy
*Medicine is a right for every needy person.*
* *"Abad Al-Rahman Pharmacy: 8 years of providing safe and free medicine."*
- Dispensing free treatments to the poor and needy all year round.
- **Average Monthly Cost:** 50,000 EGP.

### Third: The Charity Medical Complex in Al-Namrouth Village
*Comprehensive medical care free of charge.*
* *"The Medical Complex: 10 years of excellence in free healthcare."*
Provides all its services for free to those unable to pay, and includes:
- Baby Incubators
- General Surgery Center
- Medical Analysis Laboratory
- More than 13 specialized medical clinics

### Fourth: Fixed Monthly Aid
- Supporting more than **250 families**.
- With a monthly cost of **200,000 EGP**.

### Fifth: General Aid
Providing urgent assistance according to the needs of the cases, including:
- Medical Treatment
- Medical Equipment
- Living Support
- Urgent Needs

## 6. Why Support Abad Al-Rahman Foundation?
*"Because we are the sustainable bridge of your trust, we deliver your giving as medicine and food to the heart of those who deserve it."*

- **A Decade of Trust and Sustainability:** We do not offer temporary solutions. We manage continuous projects (the medical complex for 10 years, and the pharmacy and kitchen for 8 years), ensuring your donation reaches its rightful place permanently.
- **Comprehensive Medical Care:** We support patients unable to pay through an integrated medical complex and a pharmacy providing free medicine, lifting the burden of illness and treatment costs.
- **The Dignity of the Needy is Our Priority:** Through our charity kitchen, we deliver organized, hot meals to the doors of beneficiaries, sparing them from waiting in lines.
- **Transparency and Honesty:** We adhere to the highest standards of accuracy in selecting deserving cases, ensuring that every penny you give is a continuous charity and has a tangible impact on a human's life.
- **Diversity of Avenues of Goodness:** By supporting the foundation, you contribute to feeding, treating, and providing relief simultaneously and under one roof.
